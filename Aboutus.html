About Us

DZ School management system is efficient software that converts the ordinary school into a smart one. We offer easy to navigate, cost & time efficient, error-free and web-based school management solutions to tackle all the hurdles of students, parents, teachers and institute faculty efficiently. Our compact school management software is a multi-purpose suite of tools containing various modules to address all your tedious tasks quickly and effectively.

DZ School

The key Features That Cater to Your Needs Are As Follows:.

  • Administrative Management- makes it easier for faculties to be more productive by digitalizing all the information
  • Student Information Management- one-click access to student’s personal to academic details
  • Library Inventory Management- instant access to all the inventory details of the library
  • Faculty Management- makes it easier to track performance, absence of faculty staff
  • Transport Management- tracking routes and schedules of transports easily
  • Exam Management- hurdle less way to monitor, check and schedule exams efficiently

How Works School Administration Software

Managing administrative data isn’t a cup of tea for any institute, with a lot of hurdles from saving files to engaging and rolling right students, it merely becomes impossible for faculty management to produce error-free and quick results. The DZ school administration software brings all the components essential for institutions to manage all the tedious work efficiently. The different components compiled together in the school administrative management system are back-office applications allowing you to run the process for various purpose.

Time Schedule

Admin can access to change the class timetable, there is no need to worry about missing any classes as the whole class is available right on the app.

Reports

Admins can find all the reports related to different modules here within a few clicks.

Notice Board

Admin can access to add the notice, there is no need to worry about any notice as the whole school is available right on the app.
